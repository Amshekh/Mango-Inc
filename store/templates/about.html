Mango Inc - Refined Tech, Ripe Possibilities



Mango Inc founded by Amshekh Labs fame Amit Shekhar is gaining tremendous popularity among the tech gadget users.Be it smartphone or laptop it's creating buzz all around. Mango Inc gadgets run on revolutionary Alphonso OS v1.0 which is incredibly optimized and offers unmatched performance for day to day multitasking.It uses the newly discovered 'Li' isotope discovered on the south pole of moon. The discovered Lithium mines were discovered by astronauts during the lunar mission of the country.This newly discovered 'Li' isotope has enabled Mango Inc to create batteries which can run for long time without creating heating issues.Mango Inc gadgets provide wide range of varieties to choose from. Founder, CTO & CEO of Mango Inc Amit Shekhar has told that the company has plans to bring Initial Public Offering (IPO) at New York Stock Exchange in the next financial year 2024-25 in a press confrence.The company aims to be $1billion enterprise by 2025.
